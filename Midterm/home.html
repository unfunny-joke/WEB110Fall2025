About Me

My name is Callie Shrum

I am a developer and designer. Currently enrolled at Johnson County Community College and majoring in Computer Information Systems, I have skills in HTML and CSS for web development, as well as C# and Python. I like to combine these skills with my love for art and design to create solutions that are not only effective, but also appealing.

I like to consider my self an outside-the-box thinker who can come up with unconventional ideas that push the boundaries (while knowing when not to go too far)

Kansas born and raised, I am a hard worker with a can-do attitude who always shoots for the stars. In my free time you can find me playing video games, listening to music, reading, playing with my cat Francis, or flexing my creative muscles in any number of ways.
