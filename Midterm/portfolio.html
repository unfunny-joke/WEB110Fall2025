The Principles of My Work

For any project I work on, there's a handful of key principles I always try to follow:

  1. Function - It needs to do the thing it's meant to
  2. Usability - The design shouldn't interfere with or detriment its function
  3. Appeal - The user should want to use it, not loath having to
  4. Aesthetics - Why not make it pretty?
